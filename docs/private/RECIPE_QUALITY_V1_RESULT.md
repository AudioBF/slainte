## Prompt summary

The new recipe prompt keeps the planned meal as the source of truth. It instructs Gemini to preserve the card name, main protein, base, and dish style when clear; avoid turning the meal into a different recipe; and prefer simple ingredients available in Dublin supermarkets such as Lidl, Tesco, Aldi, and Dunnes.

It now explicitly prefers practical Brazilian Portuguese copy, simple proteins/carbs/vegetables, no gourmet drift, no long marinades, no advanced techniques, default `servings: 1`, up to 8 ingredients, 4–7 clear steps, no step numbering inside text, and plausible integer macros close to the planned meal card.

## Smoke script behaviour

Hard-fails on:

- non-OK HTTP status;
- invalid JSON or `ok !== true`;
- missing required fields;
- empty `ingredients`;
- empty `steps`;
- missing or non-numeric macro fields.

Warns (non-blocking) on:

- more than 8 ingredients;
- fewer than 4 or more than 7 steps;
- step text starting with numbering;
- `servings !== 1` when the planned meal does not imply meal-prep/lote;
- calories outside ±10%;
- protein below 85% of target in a hypertrophy scenario;
- carbs/fat outside ±15%;
- possible gourmet/rare ingredients;
- vague quantities such as `a gosto` without a number.
